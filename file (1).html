Happy Birthday, Anike 🤍

A story of love, grace, and joy

Anikeeee, Teminikan. My baby. Ololufemi. Idunnu mi. Enibiokan mi.

You are everything these names say you are—my sweetness, my comfort, my joy, my one and only.

I am so proud of the woman you are—the grace you carry, the strength you wear so quietly, the warmth you give so freely to everyone around you.

And I am even more in awe of the woman you are becoming... growing, glowing, blooming in ways that leave me grateful just to witness you.

Having you in my life is a gift I do not take lightly. You make ordinary moments feel like something worth remembering. You steady me. You inspire me. You make me want to be better, and that is one of the most beautiful things a person can ever do for another.

I love you more than these words can hold, and every day I am reminded why.

On this day and always, I pray that Allah continues to fill your path with peace and tranquility—that every season of your life reflects His mercy, His light, and His unending favour upon you.

May you always know rest in your heart, happiness in your home, and love wherever you go.

Happy Birthday, my love. This one is all yours 🤍

I love youuuuuuuuuuuuu ♾️

Made with endless love by You 💖
